Web Forms

Forms are what make the World Wide Web viable for commerce.  It is the business side of the Web.  Pay attention, here is where the $$$ is made!

 

At the end of this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Construct and test HTML forms.
  • Identify ways that CGI scripts can parse and transmit information from a form, including e-mail, FTP, HTTP, HTTPS.
  • Activate features provided by managed services (e.g., CGI, forms, e-mail, HTTP, HTTPS).

 

Electronic textbook reading:  Site Development Associate, Lesson 6 Web.Forms.

To demonstrate your competency with these topics you will:

  • Create an simple mailto: guestbook form with:
    • text fields
    • list fields
    • text areas
  • Take a quiz about HTML forms
