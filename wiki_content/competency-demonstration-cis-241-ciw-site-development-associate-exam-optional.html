CIW Site Development Associate Exam ID: 1D0-61B, Optional

To further demonstrate your competency of the topics of these modules: 

You can take and pass the CIW Site Development Associate Exam ID: 1D0-61B (optional, not required)

OPTIONAL - CIW Site Development Associate Exam ID: 1DO-61B

This allows you to get the CIW Site Development Associate industry certification.  In order to take this exam, it is strongly recommended that you complete the CIW online curriculum and take the practice exam, passing it at a 90% or better rate before scheduling your industry exam. 

 

By passing the CIW Site Development Associate Exam ID: 1D0-61B, you will have demonstrated your ability to:

:
  • use X/HTML, HTML5, CSS 1, 2, and 3 to develop Web pages and sites
  • use digital media within a web site

 

The cost of taking the CIW exam 1D0-61B is $40 at Edmonds Community College (SNH-0123).  The exam is usually taken in the last two weeks (Nov 27, 2017 to Dec 8, 2017) of the quarter but you can take it any time of the quarter.  Contact the instructor for an appointment for the exam.  The Exam Retake Policy states that if a candidate fails the first attempt, the candidate must wait a minimum of 24 hours before a second attempt at the exam (each attempt will cost $40). If the candidate fails the second attempt, he or she must wait 30 days before retaking the exam.

 

The CIW exam 1D0-61B is based on the material of CIW Site Development Associate online curriculum.  You should study Lesson 1 to 10 to prepare for the exam.

You can find out more information about the CIW Site Development Associate course and exam at

http://www.ciwcertified.com/certifications/web_foundations_series/sda.php

 Verifying valid identification
To be admitted to the CTC (Certification Testing Center), each candidate must bring two forms of identification as positive proof of identity. One form of ID must bear a recent photograph, and both must bear the candidate's signature.

Confirm that the candidate has brought acceptable ID to the CTC.
Acceptable forms of photo identification include the following:
• Driver's license
• Passport
• State ID card
• Military ID card
• Employee ID card
• Student ID card

Acceptable forms of non-photo identification include the following:
• Credit card
• Check-cashing card

Unacceptable forms of identification include the following:
• Social Security card
