Global Environment

We're not in Kansas anymore, Toto.

At the end of the lesson, you should be able to:

  • Identify issues related to working in a global environment, including different currencies, multi-lingual issues, international shipping, legal and regulatory issues.
  • Identify the importance of SSL/TLS to a transaction that contains sensitive information.

 

Be sure to clearly state an organization's geographical limitations.  Are sales available globally?   Recently searching for a different taste in pizza to order online, I found something that looked wonderful.  I place an order for delivery, not noticing that the organization was Lynwood, CA instead Lynnwood, WA.  The phone call was interesting and the fact that there was a charge on my credit card was very difficult to deal with.  By the way, I still get emails from the pizza parlor in Lynwood, CA.

Always secure any transaction information via SSL or TLS.  Information is transported across the Internet in packets.  Packet listeners are out there looking for any set of digits that is formatted 4-4-4-4.
